About Me

My academic journey led me to the halls of Rutgers University, where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Health Administration. My professional path has been intertwined with the intricate world of Revenue Cycle Management (RCM), boasting a rich six-year history within the healthcare domain. My most recent venture finds me as an RCM Analyst at a health technology firm, an experience that kindled my passion for software.

Navigating across departments, I stumbled upon the captivating realm of web development. The allure of crafting digital experiences propelled me to reshape my career trajectory. As a dedicated coding bootcamp student, I'm embarking on a journey to grasp the intricacies of full-stack web development. My aspiration is to weave together my diverse experiences into the fabric of a skilled web developer.

Eager to embrace the world of coding, I'm poised to unlock new horizons upon completing this transformational journey. Armed with my past in healthcare and a burgeoning passion for web development, I envision a future where I can leverage my skills to create impactful digital solutions.
